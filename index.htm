Asia's FAMOUS GAME

PUBG

1]INFORMATION

PUBG: Battlegrounds (PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds) is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game developed by PUBG Studios and published by Krafton. In this game, up to 100 players land on a large map using parachutes and search for weapons, armor, and supplies to survive. As the match continues, the safe zone gradually shrinks, forcing players to move closer and fight each other. Players can choose to play solo, in pairs (duo), or in teams of four (squad). The main goal is to be the last player or team alive, which is celebrated as “Winner Winner Chicken Dinner.” PUBG is known for its realistic graphics, strategic gameplay, and intense combat, and it is available on PC, consoles, and as PUBG Mobile, making it one of the most popular battle royale games worldwide

2]GAME POPULARITY

PUBG: Battlegrounds is one of the most popular battle royale games in the world because it introduced a realistic and competitive survival style of gameplay that attracted millions of players. After its release, PUBG quickly gained global attention, especially among young gamers, due to its intense matches, teamwork-based play, and large open maps. The launch of PUBG Mobile further increased its popularity by making the game accessible to smartphone users, particularly in countries like India, China, and Southeast Asia. PUBG’s popularity is also boosted by its strong esports presence, regular updates, new maps, and seasonal events, which keep players engaged. Streaming platforms and gaming influencers have also played a major role in promoting PUBG, helping it remain a well-known and widely played game even years after its release
